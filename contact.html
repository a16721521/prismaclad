FAQ

Things Worth Knowing

These cover the most common questions about the work, the process, and how projects get started.

If your question is not here, use the contact form above. We respond to every inquiry and are happy to get into more detail on anything that is specific to your project or site.

What types of buildings do you work on?

Data centers primarily, and any large-scale industrial or infrastructure building: distribution facilities, utility structures, manufacturing plants. If the building is large, blank, and needs to belong somewhere, we can help.

What problem does this actually solve?

The exterior problem. Not noise, water, or grid. The thing everyone sees immediately. It is the cheapest problem on the list and unlike the others, it has a straightforward fix.

Does this disrupt construction?

Each project be sequenced around construction milestones for new builds, or applied directly to existing structures with no construction disruption.

Do you handle permitting?

Yes. We support the permitting process as part of every project, including documentation for planning board submissions.

Can you work across a portfolio?

Yes. We develop design systems that adapt site to site without starting from scratch each time.

How do we get started?

Fill out the contact form. Every project starts with a conversation about the site, the timeline, and what you're trying to solve.
