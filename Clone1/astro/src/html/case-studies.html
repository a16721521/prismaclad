Common Questions & Answers

What developers and project teams typically ask before engaging Prismaclad.

Prismaclad designs, produces, and installs large-scale painted and vinyl exterior treatments for data centers and industrial buildings. We handle the full process from site survey through installation under one contract.

We work on tilt-up concrete, metal panel, and masonry exterior structures. Both new construction (design can begin at permit stage) and standing buildings (including occupied sites). Scale ranges from single buildings to developer portfolios.

Tilt-up concrete, metal panel, and masonry exterior surfaces. Both smooth and textured. We'll confirm compatibility at the site survey stage.

Survey to installed treatment typically runs 10–16 weeks depending on site complexity and production schedule. Portfolio-scale projects are sequenced to match your development timeline.
