Why Facade Treatment

Counties now write aesthetic requirements directly into data center approvals.

Landscaping takes decades and fails inspection. Full architectural redesign costs tens of millions. Large-scale exterior treatment is what fits between those two options.

  • Satisfies aesthetic ordinance requirements
  • Applied to new construction or standing structures
  • Portfolio-scale delivery under one contract
  • No construction disruption on occupied sites

“The aesthetic requirements in these ordinances are written to be vague. Our job is to turn that vagueness into a design brief — and deliver something that passes review.”

Marco Rowling
Founder of Cedar
