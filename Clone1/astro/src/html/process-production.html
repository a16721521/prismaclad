Carbon Footprint & Net Zero

Measure, reduce, and offset your carbon emissions with science-based methodologies that provide the data foundation for credible climate action, net-zero commitments, and climate-resilient positioning.

Carbon is a strategic business issue affecting cost of capital, market access, customer relationships, regulatory exposure, and physical asset values. SBTi has mobilized thousands toward reduction pathways. CSRD requires granular Scope 3 data. Investors and talent judge organizations by the credibility of their climate commitments.

Our Methodology

We follow the GHG Protocol Corporate Standard across all three scopes:

Scope 1: Direct emissions from owned sources — fuel combustion, process emissions, fugitive refrigerants.

Scope 2: Indirect emissions from purchased electricity, steam, heating, and cooling.

Scope 3: Value chain emissions across 15 categories — purchased goods, transportation, business travel, use of sold products, and more. Often the largest portion of an organization's footprint.

From Footprint to Net Zero

Science-Based Targets: We prepare SBTi submissions with the right target type, base year, and scope coverage — guiding organizations through validation.

Abatement Strategy: We develop a cost curve ranking every reduction initiative by cost per tonne of CO₂e avoided, so you prioritize actions delivering the biggest reductions for the lowest cost.

Transition Planning: Multi-year roadmaps with quick wins (efficiency, renewables), medium-term investments (process changes, fleet electrification), and long-term transformations (business model innovation, circular economy).

Ongoing Monitoring: Tracking systems, TCFD-aligned disclosures, and CDP preparation — making climate data part of your regular business rhythm.
