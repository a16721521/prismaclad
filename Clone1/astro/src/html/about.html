About Us

About Us

Prismaclad specializes in one thing: large-scale exterior treatments for industrial facilities. Our work sits between landscaping and architecture — faster than a redesign, longer-lasting than planting, and purpose-built to satisfy the aesthetic requirements counties now write into data center approvals.

Core Philosophy

We design from the building out — not from a pattern library. Every treatment starts with the actual structure: its scale, surface type, site context, and the specific language in the applicable ordinance.

How we work

A clear path from site survey to installed treatment.

Prismaclad manages each project end-to-end — survey, design, production, and installation under one contract.

01

Survey

We visit the site, document the structure, and gather the applicable ordinance language. Every design decision follows from what the building actually is.

02

Design

Custom patterns developed from your site parameters — scale, surface, surroundings, and the specific aesthetic requirements in your county's approval conditions.unities that matter most to your organization.

03

Build

We create a practical ESG roadmap, reporting structure, and action plan aligned with your goals and available resources.

04

Measure

We help you track progress, improve disclosures, and communicate measurable outcomes with clarity and confidence.
