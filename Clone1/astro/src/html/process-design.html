Sustainability Reporting & Disclosure

Navigate the complex landscape of sustainability reporting frameworks with confidence. We help you produce transparent, credible reports that meet investor expectations, satisfy regulatory requirements, and tell your authentic sustainability story.

With CSRD mandating detailed ESRS disclosures, SEC climate rules finalized, and ISSB creating global baseline standards, your sustainability report is one of your most scrutinized communications. It needs to be accurate, comprehensive, and genuinely reflective of your performance.

What We Do

Framework Navigation: We help you determine which frameworks apply — GRI, SASB, ISSB, CSRD/ESRS, TCFD, CDP, UN Global Compact — and build a unified data collection system feeding multiple outputs from a single source of truth.

Data Collection & Validation: We design workflows that reach the right people, include validation checks at each step, and provide calculation methodologies for complex metrics like Scope 3 emissions and supply chain due diligence.

Story Development: Our writing team crafts narratives that explain why numbers look the way they do, what you're doing about them, and where you're headed — turning data into compelling stories without sacrificing rigor.

Assurance Coordination: We prepare evidence packs, respond to verifier queries, and coordinate with assurance providers to make the process smoother and more likely to result in unqualified opinions.

Beyond the Annual Report: Integrated reporting, real-time digital disclosure, investor-specific communications, and internal board-level dashboards that turn sustainability data into decision-making tools.

Deliverables:

  • Publishable Sustainability Report (Annual/Integrated)
  • Framework Crosswalk & Alignment Map
  • Data Collection Templates & Protocols
  • Visualizations, Infographics & Data Stories
  • Management Letter with Improvement Recommendations
  • Stakeholder Q&A Talking Points

Included Services:

  • GRI Standards Reporting (Universal, Sector, Topic)

  • TCFD Climate Disclosure
